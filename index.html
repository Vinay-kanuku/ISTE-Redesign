About ISTE GRIET

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is a national, non-profit professional organization that evolved from the Association of Principals of Technical Institutions (APTI) in 1968. Its primary goal is to contribute to the development of high-quality engineers and technicians needed by industry. The ISTE chapter at GRIET was established in 2004 and has been actively organizing co-curricular activities that complement the changing educational curriculum. These events provide students with opportunities to compete with peers, apply their learning practically, and develop technical skills that help them achieve their career goals.

Vision

"ISTE's Vision is that all educators are empowered to harness technology to accelerate innovation in teaching and learning, and inspire learners to reach their greatest potential.."

Mission

ISTE inspires educators worldwide to use technology to innovate teaching and learning, accelerate good practice and solve tough problems in education by providing community, knowledge and the ISTE Standards, a framework for rethinking education and empowering learners.
